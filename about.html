Why Wavecom?

I created Wavecom because most Material Design frameworks before were not flexible enough and they were not human-friendly. With Wavecom I hope to give people an easier way to create websites with a beautiful aesthetic.


Speed

Wavecom is built over a lightweight CSS and Javascript base sprinkled with some Jquery for some magic.

Semantic

All components were built to be semantic in terms of attributes: [btn], [bar], [content] and the likes.
